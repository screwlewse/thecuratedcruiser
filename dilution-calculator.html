Professional Dilution Guide

  • 1:1 - Tire dressing, extreme wheel cleaning situations
  • 2:1 - Exterior trim dressing, wheel wells, engine bay dressing
  • 4:1 - Standard wheel cleaner dilution
  • 5:1 - Foam cannon soap, fabric/carpet cleaners
  • 10:1 - Interior all-purpose cleaners (standard strength)
  • 20:1 - Diluted interior cleaner (for sensitive surfaces)
  • 32:1 - Quick detailers, rinseless wash, clay lubricant (ONR)
Remember: Always test products in an inconspicuous area first!
